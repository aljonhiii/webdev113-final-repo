About Us

Community Issue-Reporting System

The Community Issue Reporting System is a digital platform designed to help citizens report, track, and resolve community concerns efficiently.

This system provides a secure and organized way for barangay residents to submit issues such as garbage disposal, flooding, street problems, health concerns, noise disturbances, or disputes. By simulating the real barangay workflow, including the Lupon dispute resolution process, it ensures accountability, transparency, and community engagement.

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between barangay officials and residents through accessible digital governance.

Our Vision

A peaceful, organized, and proactive community where every citizen's voice is heard and acted upon.
